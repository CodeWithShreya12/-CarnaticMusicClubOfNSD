Our Journey

Founded by Shreya in 2022, the Carnatic Music Club began at Canyon Creek Elementary with just five students. Over the years, it has grown into a thriving district-wide initiative. Today, the club reaches 25–30 students annually, and has expanded to Fernwood Elementary, with plans for further district-wide expansion. It promotes Indian classical music and nurtures young talent through community-led instruction.
