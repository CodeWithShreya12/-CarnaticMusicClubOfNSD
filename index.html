Our Journey

In 2022, a passionate high school student named Shreya Battula envisioned a way to share her love for Carnatic music with the younger generation in her community. What began as a small after-school club at Canyon Creek Elementary—with just five curious students—soon blossomed into something far greater. With dedication, heart, and the power of music, the idea grew into a district-wide initiative that now proudly introduces this rich tradition of Indian classical music to interested students. This journey wouldn't have been possible without the unwavering support of Mr. Srinivas Khedam and Mr. Elliot Gray (Canyon Creek’s Music and Marimba teacher). From the early days of planning to the club’s flourishing success, their guidance has been instrumental every step of the way. What started as a humble spark has now become a shining example of how one student’s passion can inspire a community.
